The Email Reply Drafter intelligently analyzes incoming emails and generates **context-aware draft replies**, saving time while ensuring high-quality and timely communication.

---

## Overview

The **Email Reply Drafter Agent** helps you:

- Monitor your Gmail inbox for new incoming emails.
- Automatically classify emails that require a reply.
- Draft professional and contextually relevant responses, considering the entire email thread history.
- Save the response as a Gmail draft, ready for manual review and sending.

This agent reduces manual effort in handling repetitive email responses, improves accuracy, and ensures consistent communication.

---

## Real-Time Use Cases

- **Customer Support Teams** – Auto-generate responses for frequently asked customer queries, reducing average response time.
- **Sales and Lead Management** – Draft professional follow-up emails for inbound leads to maintain engagement.
- **Internal Communications** – Handle repetitive requests (e.g., meeting confirmations, document sharing).
- **Freelancers and Consultants** – Save time replying to inquiries while maintaining a professional tone.

## QuickStart

Follow these steps to set up the Email Reply Drafter Agent.

### Prerequisites

- An Activepieces account (Sign up here: https://activepieces.com)
- A Gmail account that you will connect to the agent
  - Reading emails
  - Creating drafts
- An OpenAI account (Sign up here) with an API key to use GPT models

---

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1 – Log in to Activepieces

- Visit [Activepieces Cloud](https://cloud.activepieces.com)
- Sign in or create a free account.

---

### Step 2 – Import Workflow from AI20Labs

1. Go to [AI20Labs Agent Directory](https://ai20-agent-directory.lovable.app/)
2. Search for **Email Reply Drafter Agent**.
3. Subscribe to the agent.
4. Click **Open Workflow Template** → **Import**.

> Note: Ensure you’re logged in to Activepieces before importing.

---

### Step 3 – Connect Gmail (Trigger Node)

- Open **Node 1 – Gmail New Email**.
- Click **Create Connection** → Select Gmail account.
- Grant required permissions.
- Set **Label = INBOX** to avoid looping issues.

---

### Step 4 – Connect OpenAI for Email Classification (Node 2)

- Open **Node 2 – Ask ChatGPT**.
- Create a new OpenAI connection with your **Private API Key**. You can get it from [OpenAI API Keys](https://platform.openai.com/account/api-keys).
- Recommended Model: **gpt-4o-mini**.

---

### Step 5 – Connect OpenAI for Draft Reply (Node 5)

- Open **Node 5 – Ask ChatGPT**.
- Use the same OpenAI connection.
- Recommended Model: **gpt-4o**.

---

### Step 6 – Send Email Draft (Node 6)

- Open **Node 6 – Gmail Send Email**.
- Uses the previous Gmail connection.

---

## How to Run This Workflow

1. **Test Each Node**:
   - For the Trigger Node, click **Load Sample Data**.
   - For other nodes, click **Test Step** to verify connections.
2. **Publish Workflow**:
   - Click **Publish** in the top-right corner of Activepieces.

---

## What Happens Next

Once published:

1. A new email arrives in your Gmail inbox.
2. The agent checks if it requires a human response.
3. If yes, a draft reply is generated using AI.
4. You can review the draft under **Gmail → Drafts** before sending.
